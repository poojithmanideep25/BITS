ğŸ€ 11. HITS â€” Hyperlink-Induced Topic Search

PageRank gives ONE score.
HITS gives TWO scores:

  • Authority score
  • Hub score

The Soul of HITS

A page is a good authority if many good hubs point TO it.
A page is a good hub if it points TO many good authorities.

Authority(p)

Sum of hub scores of pages pointing TO p.

Hub(p)

Sum of authority scores of pages p points TO.

Mutual Reinforcement

  • Hubs boost authorities.
  • Authorities boost hubs.
